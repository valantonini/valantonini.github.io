Checkout the latest website I have made for All Family Matters , a specialist psychology practice dedicated to enhancing the emotional and psychological well being of children, young people and families

The site was designed by David Ryan and assembled by myself. I started developing it with Bootstrap 2.3.2 but updated it to Bootstrap 3 when it was officially released as I thought the flat style of Bootstrap 3 aligned better with the site's design. I really enjoyed the extra control you have with the layout based on device size and continue to enjoy Bootstrap's support for IE8. I look forward to designing more sites with it in the future.
